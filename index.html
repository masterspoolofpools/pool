Rules & Scoring

Everything you need to know. Simple format, maximum drama.

📋 Entries

  • Entry is $20 per team
  • Submit as many teams as you like
  • All teams must be submitted before Thursday tee-off
  • E-transfer payment to masterspoolofpools@gmail.com
  • More entries = more chances to win!

🏆 Scoring

Points are awarded to each golfer on your team based on their final tournament finish. Your team score is the sum of all your golfers' points.

🥇 Win (1st place)+8 pts
🥈 Top 3+5 pts
Top 10+3 pts
Top 25+2 pts
Made Cut+1 pt
Missed Cut / DNS / WD / DQ-1 pt

⛳ Salary Cap

Each team has 50 "dollars" to spend. Pick as many golfers as you like as long as their total salary ≤ 50. Salaries are based on DraftKings rates. You don't have to spend the full 50.

💰 Payout

  • 100% of entry fees returned as prizes
  • Winner receives ~25% of the pot
  • As many prize positions as possible
  • Tied teams in the money share the average of positional payouts
  • $40 deducted from pot for website costs
