Rules & Scoring

Everything you need to know. Simple format, maximum drama.

📋 Entries

  • Entry is $20 per team
  • Submit as many teams as you like
  • All teams must be submitted before Thursday tee-off
  • E-transfer payment to masterspoolofpools@gmail.com

🏆 Scoring

Points awarded based on each golfer's final tournament finish. Your team score is the sum of all your golfers' points.

🥇 Win (1st place)+8 pts
🥈 Top 3+5 pts
Top 10+3 pts
Top 25+2 pts
Made Cut+1 pt
Missed Cut / DNS / WD / DQ−1 pt

⛳ Salary Cap

  • Each team has 50 "dollars" to spend
  • Pick as many golfers as you like as long as their total salary ≤ 50
  • Salaries are based on DraftKings rates and are now locked. Note that some players listed (such as Tiger and Phil) may not be in the field.
  • You don't have to spend the full 50
